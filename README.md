Problem Statement: Inefficient inventory management leads to significant food waste, financial loss, and environmental strain for small-to-medium business (SMB) owners.
The Solution: A predictive dashboard that uses AI to forecast stockouts, suggest contextual reorders, and bridge the gap between surplus and community need. To modernize the inventory pipeline for SMB owners by shifting from reactive restocking to proactive, AI-driven waste prevention. We aim to maximize profit while minimizing environmental footprint.
